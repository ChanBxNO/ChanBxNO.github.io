Homemade French Fries

Info

  • Calories: 357
  • Servings: 1
  • Time to prepare: 50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Baking Patato
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • barbecue sauce
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Wash potato
  2. Pre-heat oven to 450 degrees
  3. Cut potato into thick wedges
  4. Mix olive oil and spices together in a bowl
  5. Coat potatoes with the oil-spice mixture
  6. Place on baking sheet
  7. Bake until golden brown for about 45 minutes
  8. Finish with salt and peper
